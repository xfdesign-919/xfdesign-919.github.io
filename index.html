About Me

Hi, I'm Xander, a Cybersecurity Analytics and Operations major at Penn State heading into my senior year. This summer I've been interning at Kodiak Gas Services, a midstream oil and gas company, rotating through the security operations center, governance and compliance, and the IT service desk. I came in expecting the work to be purely technical. The biggest lesson so far has been the opposite: a detection means nothing until someone can explain it clearly to the person who has to act on it. Good security turns out to be a lot about good communication.

Outside of the internship, I like to build things. I designed Onestep, an iOS app that uses friction to interrupt compulsive screen time, and a macOS automation system I put together largely by directing and pressure-testing AI output rather than trusting it outright. I've also built a relational database system for hospital recordkeeping as a personal project. When I'm not at a screen, I'm usually lifting, playing basketball, or keeping my Jeep alive through a Texas summer.

What I value most is follow-through, actually finishing what I plan rather than just sketching it out. After graduation, I'm aiming for a role in security operations or AI security, somewhere I can keep building things that protect people. This site is where I'm keeping track of what I'm learning along the way.
