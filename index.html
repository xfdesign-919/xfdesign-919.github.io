About Me

Hi, I'm Xander, a Cybersecurity Analytics and Operations major at Penn State heading into my senior year. This summer I've been interning at Kodiak Gas Services, a midstream oil and gas company, rotating through the security operations center, governance and compliance, and the IT service desk. I came in expecting the work to be purely technical. But the biggest lesson so far has been the opposite: a detection means nothing until someone can explain it clearly to the person who has to act on it. Good security has a lot to do with and about good communication.

Outside of the internship, I like to build things. I'm designing Onestep, an iOS app that uses friction to interrupt compulsive screen time, and M.B.A.S., a macOS automation system that delivers a personalized morning briefing using launchd scheduling, calendar and weather API integrations, and a custom dashboard. I've also built a relational database system for hospital recordkeeping as a personal project. When I'm not at a screen, I'm usually lifting, playing basketball, or keeping my old Jeep for offroading adventures.

What I value most is follow-through - actually finishing what I plan rather than just sketching it out. After graduation, I'm aiming for a role in security operations or AI security, somewhere I can keep building things that protect people. This site is where I'm keeping track of what I'm learning along the way.
